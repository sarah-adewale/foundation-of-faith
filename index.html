The Foundation of Faith program holds a session in each quarter of the year Online and at all 3 centres of the Church; Iganmu, Yaba & Lekki.
A session consists of seven classes covering the topics listed above.
An attendee is expected to go through all seven classes to have completed the program. However it is not required that the classes must be completed in a session.
If for any reason an attendee cannot attend one or more classes in a session, the attendee can take those classes in a subsequent session.
