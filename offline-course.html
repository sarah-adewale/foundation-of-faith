Courses

Browse Offline Courses

New Creation Realities
This class will help the student to understand God's gift of salvation. It brings to light the condition of the unsaved man and the immeasurable love of God demonstrated in the sacrifice of Jesus to make salvation available to all. The course concludes with lessons on the identity, benefits, rights and privileges of the man who is now in Christ.
Integrity of God's Word
This class shows the infallibility of God's Word. The student will learn how the Word of God has the same attributes as God; powerful, faithful, true and dependable. It also shows how the believer's life is transformed as they fill their hearts with God's Word.
The Holy Spirit
How much do you know about the Holy Spirit and His role in the life of the believer? This class sheds light on the person of the Holy Spirit. It discusses the role of the Holy Spirit in the life of the believer; what it means to be filled with the Spirit and how to maximize our relationship with the Holy Spirit.
What is Faith
In this class you will get a good understanding of the subject of Faith, how faith comes and how you can apply your faith to everyday life.

More Courses

Praise and Worship
In this class we shall be considering praise and worship as an outflow of our relationship with God. We'll see how every new testament believer has been made a priest, what our priestly service entails and how to effectively integrate the practice of this truth into our daily lives.
Prayer
While prayer is a very popular subject, much about it is still shrouded in an air of mystery. This class will answer such questions as what is Prayer and what is the purpose of prayer. The course will also show how to have an effective Prayer Life by discussing different kinds of prayers and how to offer them. The student will come to understand why a life of prayer is so important and how to cultivate one.
Love
A comprehensive study of the Bible reveals that man exists because of love, the love of God. God didn't need to create man, or redeem him after the fall, or give him the promise of glory but He did them all because of love. This class shows how this love nature of God has become the nature of the believer. We'll reflect on how to walk in Love and the result this produces in our lives.
