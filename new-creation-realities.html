  • Overview

Class Objective

This class will help you understand God's gift of salvation. It brings to light the condition of the unsaved man and the immeasurable love of God demonstrated in the sacrifice of Jesus to make salvation available to all. The course concludes with lessons on the identity, benefits, rights and privileges of the man who is now in Christ.
