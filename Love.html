Course Objective

A comprehensive study of the Bible reveals that man exists because of love, the love of God. God didn't need to create man, or redeem him after the fall, or give him the promise of glory but He did them all because of love. This class shows how this love nature of God has become the nature of the believer. We'll reflect on how to walk in Love and the result this produces in our lives.
